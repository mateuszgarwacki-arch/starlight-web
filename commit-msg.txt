S44a — Add unassigned items to existing WO

New flow on the scope page Unassigned Items panel: when items are
selected, a second button "Add to existing WO" appears alongside the
existing "Create WO from N". Picks a live WO (excludes Voided/Complete/
Cancelled) showing activity label, status, current item count and hours
logged. Inserts into tbl_jobitem_workorder, deduped against existing
junctions client-side (no DB-level UNIQUE on the junction table). Fires
a scope_change notification to anyone watching the target WO. PM gets a
toast; panel refreshes and expands the target WO.

New component:  src/components/add-to-wo-dialog.tsx
Touched:        src/components/work-orders-panel.tsx
