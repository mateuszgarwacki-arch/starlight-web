fix(labels): rotate label content 90° for portrait-fed GT800 (S53)

Print output came out sideways relative to the physical 2x1 label. The
GT800 feeds the 1in edge first, so the page is now portrait (1in x 2in)
and the 2x1 content is rotated 90° into it via a .label-inner wrapper
(absolute-centred, transform rotate(90deg)). Rotation is print-only —
the on-screen preview stays upright and readable. If it comes out 180°
off, flip rotate(90deg) -> rotate(-90deg).
