S44b — Fixings & Consumables BOM type

New 4th button on the scope-level Build Plan ("Add Fixings"), opening a
catalogue picker filtered to stock items tagged item_type='fixing', with
a freeform custom-entry mode fallback. Qty field defaults blank — blank
persists as NULL, which is the "needs this, not counted" mode for
consumables (sandpaper, masking tape, drill bits). Counted fixings carry
a real qty for the build-time pick-list. Inserts land at scope level
(work_order_id NULL) with material_category = 108.
