S30 Phase 2: Job-level and scope-level document panels

- WODocumentsPanel gains a job-level query branch: when neither WO nor
  scope is passed, loads docs anchored directly at the job (scope_item_id
  and work_order_id both null)
- cut_list section hidden at non-WO mounts (the extractor is tightly
  coupled to a single WO's BOM)
- Mounted on /jobs/[id] as a new "Job Documents" card after the
  invoices/orders block — site plans, master CAD, job-wide references
- Mounted on /jobs/[id]/scope/[scopeId] as "Scope Documents" card after
  the learnings section — scope CAD concept/breakdown, scope drawings

No inheritance: each anchor level shows only its own docs. Mobile
freelancer view, traveller PDF, and WO panel are unaffected (they
filter by work_order_id).

PM 100m view's rpc_pm_job_overview already surfaces scope-anchored
docs under their parent quote line (via tbl_scope_items join) — so
scope-level uploads will appear in PM view automatically. Job-level
docs stay off the PM view by design; they're browsed via the admin
Job Documents card, and Phase 3's /library/cad will handle cross-job
discovery.

Known gaps (deferred to cleanup backlog):
- Job-level filename collisions: no activity/scope prefix, same filename
  twice overwrites (OneDrive conflictBehavior=replace)
- WODocumentsPanel still uses raw supabase.insert() not auditedInsert
