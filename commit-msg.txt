margin against it. For jobs where the workshop delivers a slice of the
event (subcontracted production, install, etc.) that margin is
meaningless — FA Leoube read 98.8% against the whole-event quote.

non-overhead lines where category NOT IN ('Subcontracted','Provisional').

category field on tbl_quote_lines is PM-maintained; exclude-list (not
include-list) so new categories default to counting as workshop.
