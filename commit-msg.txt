Workshop Inbox: editable hours before approve/route

Problem: PM had to approve a task first, then leave the inbox, hunt
down the resulting entry, and edit the hours separately. With the
phantom-timer patrol now auto-closing forgotten timers to 0h, this
shape became actively painful — every patrolled task arrives in the
inbox needing an hours correction.

Fix:
- Inline editable hours input on every task row (replaces read-only
  claimed_hours span). Decimal hours, 0.25 step.
- Amber border + "edited" tag when value differs from claimed_hours
  so the override is visible at a glance.
- Approve Overhead now writes the effective hours into tbl_tasks.hours
  and reports it in the toast + notification.
- Route to WO carries the edited value through to RouteTaskModal.
- Reject ignores hours (task is rejected — hours irrelevant).

Audit gap closed at the same time:
- handleApproveOverhead and handleRejectTask switched from raw
  supabase.update() to auditedUpdate(). tbl_tasks is in
  AUDITED_TABLES; the prior raw calls were a violation of the
  "never raw supabase.update() on key tables" rule. Hours edits
  in particular are pay-affecting and must be audited.
