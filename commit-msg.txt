fix(s28d): PM view — missing WO-anchored BOM rows + timber Length/Metre cost bug

Two correctness bugs surfaced by Ballroom Reception (scope 17):

1) BOM rows anchored only to a work_order (scope_item_id IS NULL) were
   silently dropped by the RPC. Polyline IFR (£243) was invisible on the
   PM view, so "Spent" under-reported materially. Fixed: bom_raw and
   docs_per_line now anchor to scope via COALESCE(b.scope_item_id,
   wo.scope_item_id) so WO-only BOM/doc rows surface through the WO.

2) Length-unit BOM rows used a per-metre unit_cost without converting,
   so a 3x2 Rounded Edge charged as "4 × £1.65 = £6.60" instead of
   "4 lengths × 4.8m × £1.65 = £31.68". Fixed via length_factor in
   bom_raw: when bom.unit='Length' AND material.unit='Metre' AND
   material.standard_length>0, multiply qty by standard_length_m.
   Applies to 19 timber rows DB-wide. Correct costs now flow into
   materials_total and the "Live spent" job strip.

Frontend:
- BomGroup type gains standard_length_m
- MaterialRow shows "4 Length (× 4.8m = 19.2m)" when conversion applies,
  so the £ number visibly ties to the original quantity

Admin-side materials display has the same bug but is NOT changed here
(different queries, out of PM-view scope). Queued as follow-up.
