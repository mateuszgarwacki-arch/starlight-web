feat(wo-docs): show stripped filename under drawing/reference thumbnails

When reordering drawings, the meaningful clue is often in the user's
original filename - but the displayed name was previously only available
on hover via title attribute, which is awkward during drag-drop reorder.

Now shows the filename as a caption under each thumbnail, with the
{activity}-{scope}- prefix stripped (those parts are already implicit
from the WO context) and the file extension removed. Hyphens converted
to spaces for readability. Caption respects doc.caption if explicitly
set, otherwise computes from file_name.

Layout: 2-line clamp, 12px muted text, centred under 176px-wide thumb.
