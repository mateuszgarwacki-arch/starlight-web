feat(pdf-viewer): route all PDFs in docs panel through in-app viewer

viewDoc() (the eye icon on cut lists and other non-grid doc types) now
opens PDFs in the PdfViewer modal instead of a new browser tab. Non-PDF
types (csv, xlsx, etc.) continue to open in a new tab as before.

Closes the inconsistency where drawings/references PDFs opened in-app
but cut list PDFs (same file type, same panel) still opened externally.
