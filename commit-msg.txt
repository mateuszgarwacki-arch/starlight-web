fix: proper 2D guillotine bin packing for sheet material calculation

Area-based sheet counting was completely wrong — a 1830x865mm part on
a 2440x1220mm sheet can only fit once (865+865>1220), but area math
said 2 sheets for 6 parts. Now uses guillotine bin packing algorithm
that places actual rectangles on sheets with rotation support,
splitting remaining space via guillotine cuts. Timber still uses 1D
FFD bin packing. All math is deterministic JS — Claude only extracts
the raw parts list.
