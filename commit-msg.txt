feat: editable dates on time entries and ad-hoc tasks

WO time entries: date now part of the pending-edit flow. Schema gets
proposed_date column on tbl_wo_time_entry_edits, and the approve RPC
rebuilds timestamps using new date + existing time-of-day so a 09:00
entry stays 09:00 on the new day. EditTimeEntrySheet enables LogSheet's
date picker pre-populated with the entry date. PM review row shows
a date diff inline when changed.

Ad-hoc tasks: date edits update worked_date directly, same approved->
pending revert when PM had already approved. EditableTask gains a
worked_date field; both /m/me call sites pass it through.

Nothing-broken invariant preserved: WO entry timestamps don't move
until you approve. Cost-by-day, margin-by-day, daily timesheet flags
all stay on the canonical date.
