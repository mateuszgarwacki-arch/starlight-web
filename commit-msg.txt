Redesign task routing modal: two-pane job + WO picker (S39b)

Replace cramped 512px flat-list modal with a 1024px two-pane layout:

- Left pane: job picker with search, task's origin job auto-selected and
  badged, sorted by event date. Shows job number, name, event date, WO
  count, and an amber coin icon marking jobs with the S39 overhead bucket.
- Right pane: WOs for the selected job. Overhead WO pinned to the top in
  a distinct amber-outlined card (one-click routing to the overhead
  bucket). Regular WOs grouped by scope below so the PM scans by build
  area, not by raw WO description.
- Removes the silent 20-WO cap by fetching all active WOs across active
  jobs up front and filtering client-side.

Extracted to src/components/route-task-modal.tsx so the inbox page stays
readable. Inbox page now renders <RouteTaskModal> when a task is being
routed; old inline modal, filteredWos derivation, and WOOption type
removed.

Addresses the UX friction where freelancer tasks with a known job_id
still required searching through a flat global list of 60+ WOs to find
the right one, and where routing to the new S39 overhead bucket required
three clicks of guesswork because it wasn't visually distinguished.
